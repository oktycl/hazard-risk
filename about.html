Our Mission

Transforming Seismic Safety

At Hazard & Risk, we believe that earthquake risk information should be accessible and actionable. Our AI-powered platform delivers professional-grade seismic risk assessments in seconds, enabling our partners to protect assets, manage portfolios, and make confident, data-driven investment decisions.

We are committed to helping our partners reduce earthquake-related losses, ensure operational continuity, and strengthen resilience by transforming complex seismic science into clear, actionable intelligence.

  • Provide enterprises with accessible seismic risk intelligence
  • Accelerate global resilience across industries and infrastructures
  • Empower organizations to protect assets and investments with actionable insights
  • Bridge the gap between scientific complexity and corporate decision-making

Powered by advanced machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of seismic data points worldwide

Our Journey

2020

Global Engineering Success

Successfully completed over 100 engineering projects across 5 continents, building strong expertise in seismic safety and resilience.

2021
