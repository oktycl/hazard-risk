Our Mission

Transforming Seismic Safety

At Hazard & Risk, we believe that everyone deserves to know their earthquake risk. Our cutting-edge AI technology democratizes access to professional-grade seismic risk assessment, making it possible to evaluate building safety in minutes rather than weeks.

We're committed to reducing earthquake-related casualties and economic losses worldwide by providing accurate, actionable insights that empower better decision-making.

  • Democratize access to earthquake risk information
  • Accelerate global seismic safety improvements
  • Empower communities with actionable insights
  • Bridge the gap between complex science and practical application

Powered by advanced machine learning algorithms trained on millions of seismic data points worldwide

Our Core Values

Scientific Accuracy

We maintain the highest standards of scientific rigor in our AI models, ensuring our assessments are based on peer-reviewed research and validated methodologies.

Accessibility

Complex earthquake science should be accessible to everyone. We translate sophisticated analysis into clear, actionable insights for all users.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in earthquake risk assessment, leveraging cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies.

Trust & Transparency

We build trust through transparency in our methodologies, clear communication of limitations, and honest reporting of confidence levels.

Global Impact

Our mission extends worldwide. We're committed to making earthquake safety improvements accessible to communities regardless of their economic status.

Continuous Learning

Our AI systems continuously learn from new data and feedback, constantly improving accuracy and expanding capabilities to serve our users better.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

Hazard & Risk was founded by a team of seismologists and AI experts with a vision to democratize earthquake risk assessment.

2021

Research & Development

Developed our proprietary AI algorithms and began training on extensive seismic datasets from around the world.

2022

First Pilot Program

Launched pilot program with insurance companies and government agencies, achieving 95% accuracy in initial assessments.

2023

Platform Launch

Officially launched our web platform and API, making earthquake risk assessment accessible to professionals worldwide.

2024

Global Expansion

Expanded services to 25+ countries and processed over 100,000 building assessments, establishing partnerships with major organizations.

2025

Innovation Continues

Launching advanced features including real-time monitoring, mobile applications, and enhanced AI capabilities.
