One Coordinate. Instant Earthquake Risk Prediction.

With just a single coordinate, our system delivers a rapid earthquake risk prediction for any building — no architectural plans or on-site inspections needed. Powered by our Hazard & Risk Engine, the assessment is based on the FEMA P-154 Rapid Visual Screening methodology, digitally adapted to provide scalable and timely predictions. Within minutes, users receive a preliminary seismic risk profile that reflects potential vulnerability in the event of a major earthquake. While not a full engineering evaluation, this lightweight and transparent approach empowers planners, insurers, and policymakers with actionable insights — especially in large-scale or data-constrained environments.
