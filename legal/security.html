Security Policy

Hazard & Risk Technologies
Effective Date: August 25, 2025

At Hazard & Risk Technologies ("Hazard & Risk"), protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our customers' data is a top priority. We recognize that our platform provides critical insights into hazard and risk intelligence, and we are committed to maintaining strong security practices to safeguard that information.

1. Infrastructure and Hosting
  • Our platform is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) across the United States and Europe.
  • AWS provides industry-leading physical and network safeguards, including ISO 27001 and SOC 2 certified infrastructure.
  • All data is stored in secure data centers with redundant systems and environmental protections.
2. Data Security

Encryption in Transit & At Rest

All data is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ for transmission and AES-256 for storage.

Access Controls

Role-based access and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) are required for all administrative accounts.

Logging and Monitoring

We monitor access and system activity to detect suspicious behavior.

3. Organizational Controls

Employee Access

Only authorized personnel have access to customer data, and all access is limited to the minimum necessary.

Security Training

Employees undergo regular security awareness training, including phishing prevention and data handling best practices.

Vendor Management

Third-party providers such as AWS, Fastspring (payment processing), and Google Analytics are carefully reviewed for security and compliance.

4. Compliance and Assurance
  • Hazard & Risk is preparing for SOC 2 Type I certification, which provides independent assurance that our security controls are designed in accordance with the Trust Services Criteria.
  • We also align our practices with GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and PIPEDA requirements to ensure global compliance.
5. Incident Response
  • We maintain an Incident Response Policy to address potential security events.
  • In the event of a confirmed data breach, affected customers will be notified promptly in accordance with applicable regulations.
6. Continuous Improvement

We continually review and improve our security practices, including periodic vulnerability assessments, access reviews, and updates to policies and procedures.
