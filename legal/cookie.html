1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to website operators.

2. Types of Cookies

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the functioning of our website, such as login sessions, security, and navigation.

Duration: Session-based (deleted when you close your browser).

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences (e.g., language, user settings) to provide a more personalized experience.

Duration: Persistent, typically up to 12 months.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., page visits, time spent, device/browser statistics).

Duration: Persistent, typically up to 12 months.
Provider: Google LLC (see Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Third-Party Cookies (Fastspring Payments)

When you make a purchase via our platform, payments are processed by Fastspring. Fastspring may set its own cookies on your device for purposes such as session management, fraud detection, and secure checkout. These cookies are controlled by Fastspring and governed by their own Privacy and Cookie Policies. For more information, please visit: https://fastspring.com/privacy/

Note: We do not use advertising, marketing, or tracking cookies from third parties.
3. How You Can Control Cookies

Since we do not use a cookie preference banner, you may control cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Delete existing cookies from your device.
  • Block new cookies from being stored.
  • Set your browser to notify you when cookies are placed.

For Google Analytics specifically, you may opt out by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
