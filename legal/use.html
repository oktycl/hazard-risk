2. Description of Services

Hazard & Risk provides geospatial hazard and risk-intelligence tools. Delivery models vary by product:

Seismic Risk Predictor

Delivered through a secure customer dashboard and REST API. Provides near-instant seismic risk predictions for individual buildings or portfolios.

Hazard Maps

Prepared manually upon customer request. These are customized site-specific hazard maps integrating soil and seismic data.

Regional Risk Maps

Prepared manually upon customer request. Provide district- or city-level risk assessments based on building-level evaluations.

Financial Loss and Downtime Prediction

Currently provided manually upon request; future integration into the customer dashboard is planned. Produces loss and downtime estimates using simplified FEMA P-58 methodologies.

New Investments Tool

Currently provided manually upon request; future dashboard integration is planned. Estimates construction costs using seismic hazard and soil conditions for early-stage planning.
