Regional Risk Maps

Risk maps for buildings of a demanded district by a policy for risk accumulation, mitigation roadmaps, inventory-scale screening, and emergency planning.

Government Applications: Comprehensive regional seismic risk assessment for urban planning, emergency response coordination, public safety resource allocation, and disaster preparedness strategies. Enables government agencies to identify high-risk areas, develop evacuation plans, optimize emergency service locations, and create evidence-based policies for building codes and zoning regulations. Essential for municipal planning departments, emergency management agencies, civil defense organizations, and public safety departments to protect citizens and critical infrastructure through informed decision-making and strategic risk mitigation planning.
