Advanced Financial Impact Assessment Technology

The Financial Loss and Downtime Prediction product combines our hybrid AI algorithms with a simplified implementation of the FEMA P-58 methodology to estimate the economic consequences of earthquakes at both the building and operational level. Designed to operate with minimal input, the system requires only the geographic coordinates of a building to automatically infer structural characteristics and hazard parameters. Based on these, it produces rapid baseline estimates of financial loss (repair costs) and downtime (expected recovery duration).

To enhance accuracy, the platform provides an interactive user adjustment interface. Since detailed knowledge of a building's interior or non-structural inventory cannot always be captured automatically, users are invited to supplement the baseline model with their own data. For example, an industrial facility can upload or enter an inventory of critical machinery, high-value equipment, or sensitive contents. Once integrated, this information enables the model to deliver far more accurate predictions of both direct repair costs and indirect business interruption impacts.

The product is designed for flexibility across user groups. Insurers gain a fast yet robust tool for underwriting, premium calculation, and claims preparedness. Asset owners and operators receive a decision-support system for understanding financial exposure and planning resilience investments. Public institutions can use aggregated results for sector-wide or region-wide loss estimation and recovery planning.

By merging AI-driven rapid screening with user-controlled refinement, the Financial Loss and Downtime Prediction product delivers both speed and adaptability. It empowers decision-makers to act with confidence—offering instant, coordinate-based loss estimates while still allowing for deeper customization to reflect the unique realities of each building or portfolio.
