Advanced Technology for Critical Decisions

Our Building Risk Report leverages advanced hybrid AI algorithms to deliver fast, high-accuracy seismic risk assessments for both individual structures and large-scale property portfolios. This technology is specifically designed for insurers, reinsurers, and asset managers who often operate with limited property data and require rapid, reliable insights to inform underwriting, investment, and risk mitigation decisions.

The system performs an AI-powered rapid visual screening, applying the FEMA P-154 methodology to calculate probability of collapse alongside key building-specific risk factors. Using only geographic coordinates as input, our algorithms detect and evaluate multiple parameters: structural system type, construction material, year built, number of stories, design code compliance, vertical and plan irregularities, adjacent building proximity (pounding risk), and soil classification derived from authoritative datasets such as USGS or detailed local geotechnical surveys. Spectral acceleration values are incorporated to ensure the seismic demand is accurately represented for each site.

For contents coverage, the platform calculates floor-level accelerations to assess the likelihood of damage to non-structural components and valuable equipment. Additional hazards, such as nearby deep excavations, are also factored into the evaluation.

Through a secure API interface, clients can submit only a building's coordinates and receive, within seconds, a comprehensive risk profile‚Äîincluding numerical collapse probability, contents vulnerability scores, and highlighted deficiencies. By combining geospatial intelligence, structural engineering principles, and AI-based detection, the Building Risk Report replaces costly, time-intensive field assessments with an instantly scalable, technology-driven solution‚Äîmaking it ideal for both single-asset evaluations and global portfolio risk analysis.
